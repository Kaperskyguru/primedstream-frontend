frequently asked questions

what is primeStream?

PrimeStream is the easiest way for you to live stream to 30+ channels at once. Stream to Youtube, Facebook, and others- at the same time. Don't worry about where you have the biggest audience, just stream anywhere.

do i need ant additional software?

You'll need an encoder to stream your screen or from an external camera or video source.

can i stream to a platform that is not on your list?

Yes you can. We support custom RTMP destinations, so you can stream to any platform which supports RTMP/RTMPS stream publishing.

will stream ing with primeStream affect the quality of my stream?

No, PrimeStream duplicates the exact signal you send, and sends it back out to all channels you have connected to your dashboard.
